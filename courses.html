Courses Offered

English Literature

Explore the world of English literature and enhance your language skills. Dive deep into classic and contemporary works, poetry, and prose.

Science & Technology

Dive into the fascinating world of science and technology. Learn about various scientific concepts, conduct experiments, and explore the latest technological advancements.

Mathematics

Develop your logical thinking and problem-solving skills with our comprehensive mathematics courses. From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, we cover it all.

Social Studies

Discover the complexities of human society, culture, and history. Our social studies courses cover a wide range of topics, including geography, history, politics, and economics.

Creative Writing

Unleash your creativity and express yourself through writing. Our creative writing courses help you develop your writing skills and explore different genres and styles.

Fine Arts

Immerse yourself in the world of visual arts. Our fine arts courses cover drawing, painting, sculpture, and other creative techniques, allowing you to unleash your artistic potential.

History

Explore the events, people, and movements that have shaped the course of human history. Our history courses delve into different periods and civilizations, providing a comprehensive understanding of the past.

Politics

Gain insights into the political systems, ideologies, and structures that govern societies around the world. Our politics courses cover topics such as political theory, international relations, and governance.

Hindi

Explore the richness of the Hindi language and literature. Our Hindi courses focus on language proficiency, comprehension, and appreciation of Hindi literature and culture. it's also included sanskrit with the grammer.
